Connections

Affordable and Clean Energy is the core to everything we do today. This involved jobs, security, climate change, food production, and more. This goal is very essential as it is connected to almost all of the other goals that the UN has put in place. Finding a more efficient, renewable and sustainable energy source is vital for other economic benefits, making more sustainable communities and becoming more flexible to climate change. Right now, there are ≈3 billion people do not have clean cooking solutions and are susceptible to deadly air pollution. As well, ≈1 billion people do not have access to electricity (50% of which are from Sub-Saharan Africa).
